A browser extension designed to help you streamline your entertainment experience across all platforms.

Stream Well works with all major streaming services, both free and paid.
With Stream Well, you can get your time back.

Features



Schedule videos to watch from any of your favourite platforms.
Appoint video limits when you need them.
Find the show or movie that's just right with extra filters.
Sync your entertainment schedule to Google Calendar.

Preview the Experience




Interactive Prototype Tips
1. Click the search bar, then filter for TV shows with a maximum of two seasons (you don't need to type, simply click on the box). Then select Dark, and click schedule. This will demonstrate the scheduling feature.
2. On the same TV search results as noted above, click on The Time Tunnel and press play. This will demonstrate the episode limit feature.
3. Click on any title already in the schedule, and note that the extension will inform you that you already have it scheduled.
4. Clicking anywhere on the page will give you highlighted blue hints if you're not sure what to click next.
